About Us

The International Student Guide Amsterdam (ISGA) provides you every year with useful information and the hottest locations of the City. The guide is produced by 25 students, both international and Dutch, of the minor programme Publishing at the University of Applied Sciences (HvA). Along with this guide comes this website as its cross-media component. It has been coded by Matthias Leuhof and has been filled with content by the Publishing students.

This year, everything is under the theme “Step into the Jungle”. We want you to discover the rich mixture of urban and nature taking place in the Dutch capital with five trails. Each trail is covering a different topic, but they are all united in the vibrant heartbeat of this wild city.

Are you in Amsterdam for the first time? Then we will give you a quick introduction to the Must-Do’s and Must-See’s of the 2017 Millennials’ capital. The Bucket List will provide you with all the things you should try in Amsterdam - a perfect way to get to know the city.

The Bar Hopping trail brings you to the thriving east of the city. Close to Amstelcampus, Amsterdam Oost is well known amongst students for its relaxing atmosphere. You can find a diverse selection of Bars, Restaurants and Pubs. Not far apart, the stops of this trail are perfectly reachable by foot.

Are your days too busy and is university clouding your head? Have a break and relieve your stress in one of the greenest cities there is. Amsterdam’s Flora and Fauna is so vibrant that you can explore it in walking distances. You might even spot some Flamingos along the way…

The Food and Finesse trail is the perfect solution if the shared kitchen in your student home is messy again. We offer you the best food markets in town. Also, there are some very fancy supermarkets and restaurants. No cooking, no problem!

The Graffiti trail does not only offer perfect selfie spots, it also showcases the source of creativity and urban culture of the north. Amsterdam Noord is known for its unique character in creative industry. Check it out, you won’t be sorry.

Begin the journey of your studies abroad and discover the diverse Amsterdam city jungle!
