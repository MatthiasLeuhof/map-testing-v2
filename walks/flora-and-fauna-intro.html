Amsterdam is lush with trees, flowers and beautiful parks. For such a small city, Amsterdam also offers wildlife, picnic places and a lot of fun water spots. Take a study-break and head out. This is the best way to discover more of Amsterdam – you will be surprised! If you’re up for a short trip into Amsterdam’s finest flora and fauna, then hit the streets with friends and get walking.
