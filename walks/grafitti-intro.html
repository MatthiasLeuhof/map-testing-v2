If you would like to see a different part of Amsterdam then take the ferry and visit NDSM, the former shipyard. Although it was a shipyard for over a decade, today it has transformed into and industrial breeding ground for young entrepreneurs. Lots of artists from all over the world have left their mark on this massive outdoor gallery. There are lots of cool coffee and food places. Also the biggest flea market in Europa is held at the NDSM.

So, what are you waiting for? Hop on the ferry!
