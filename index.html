About us

Manual

This mobile website is created for the International Student Guide Amsterdam (ISGA). With the help of this website, you can walk the trails described in the guide. You can choose the trail that you want to walk and then simply follow the instructions on this website. We give you the route, background information, hotspots and the best places to eat, drink, and relax for a bit. Important is that you stay aware of your surroundings. Although you need the directions on your phone to walk the trails, be aware of the traffic around you.
